Frequently Asked Questions

Standard sessions are 60 minutes, but I also offer 90-minute sessions for deeper dives into complex topics. We can customize session length based on your needs.

Yes! All tutoring is conducted online via Zoom. Online sessions work extremely well thanks to screen sharing tools and digital whiteboards, which actually maximize our time together.

I require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be charged at the full rate. This ensures I can offer that time to other students.
