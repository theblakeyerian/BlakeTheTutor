Frequently Asked Questions

How long are tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes, but I also offer 90-minute sessions for deeper dives into complex topics. We can customize session length based on your needs.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes! I offer online (via Zoom)tutoring for students. Online sessions work great and include screen sharing and digital whiteboards that maximize efficacy.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

I require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less notice will be charged at the full rate.

Can you help with homework and test prep?

Absolutely! I help with homework, test preparation, exam review, and long-term skill building. We'll focus on whatever helps you succeed most.

Do packages expire?

5-session packages and greater do not expire!

What makes your tutoring effective?

I focus on true understanding, not just memorization. We work through problems together, identify knowledge gaps, and build problem-solving skills that last beyond the exam.
