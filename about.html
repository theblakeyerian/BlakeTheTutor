About Me

Hi, I’m Blake — a dedicated tutor passionate about making complex topics clear and approachable. Over the years, I’ve worked with students from high school to university levels, helping them master tough material while building lasting confidence.

My teaching philosophy is simple: every student can succeed with the right guidance, patience, and problem-solving strategies. I strive to create a welcoming space where students feel comfortable asking questions and engaging deeply with their studies.
